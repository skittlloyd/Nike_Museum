Nike Waffle Trainer (1974)

- Created with Bowerman’s famous waffle iron-inspired sole for better traction.

Nike Tailwind (1978)

- Introduced Nike’s revolutionary Air technology with its cushioned sole.

Nike Air Force 1 (1982)

- A groundbreaking basketball shoe and a cultural icon in fashion.

Nike Dunk (1985)

- Popular in basketball and skateboarding communities.

Nike Air Jordan 1 (1985)

- The first signature shoe for Michael Jordan, a game-changer in sports and fashion.
